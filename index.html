WORKS

These aren't strictly architecture.

This is the work that sits in between — creative experiments that cross every single discipline. Each piece came out of a way of thinking through something, understanding something, or becoming a part of myself I hadn't met yet.

I believe the best designers are shaped by more than their field.

Seedivity is an art installation merging neuroscience and design to visualize the creative process. It taught me that innovation isn't accidental, but a structured journey from chaos to tangible results.

The cardboard tube project was a practical lesson in forming sculptures. My biggest challenge was balancing the different sizes and lengths, ensuring a smooth and weighted visual flow throughout the piece. It taught me how to control visual elements to achieve balance.

This landscape painting was a pivotal moment, marking a deep dive into meditation. It transformed my perspective, teaching me to see nature with a sense of wonder and contemplation I hadn't realised was possible, revealing the importance of looking with a truly different eye.
